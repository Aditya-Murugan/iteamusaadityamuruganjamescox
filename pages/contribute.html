Get Involved

As Utah’s only Pet Partners® Community Partner, all of our training classes and team evaluations are conducted by licensed and experienced Pet Partners volunteers and evaluators. If you have your own appropriate companion animal – dog, cat, rabbit, guinea pig, domestic rat, hook-billed bird, pot-bellied pig, horse or llama you can join us to become registered as a Pet Partner Team. By joining TAU and Pet Partners you will be covered by Pet Partner’s liability insurance policy whenever and where ever you volunteer with your “Pet Partner®”.
